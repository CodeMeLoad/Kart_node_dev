Formula Design Challenge 2015
28 Jan 2015

FDC ends on a high note with the first runners up position in Business Presentation and Cost Report. 2nd in Business Plan Presentation. 2nd in Cost and Manufacturing Report

We would like to thank our institute, sponsors and alumni for believing in us and constantly supporting us.

Although a fatal engine seizure prevented us from taking part in the dynamic events nonetheless it was a great learning experience for the whole team especially due to the knowledge and the feedback given to us by Claude Rouelle, Pat Clarke, Ian Thompson, Felix and Maria.

Thank You Formula Design Challenge for giving us the much required exposure and the opportunity to showcase our engineering skills at a national platform.

Congratulations to our friends at Orion Racing India, Formula Manipal and Team Pravega for their terrific performance and a great show. Learnt a great deal from you guys!

Thank You JK TYRES, looking forward to FDC 2016!
