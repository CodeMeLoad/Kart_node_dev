Formula Design Challenge 2015
TeamKART put in a fantastic effort over 20th to 25th Jan’15 in Formula Design Challenge 2015, bagging 2nd position in Business Plan Presentation and Cost Report Analysis. Our team was one of the nine teams(out of 37) which qualified all the scrutiny tests. Although, engine seizure refrained us from participating in dynamic events, the team believes that the trip was quite successful with a lot of learning experiences.
We would like to extend a huge thanks to our Sponsors, Alumnus of IIT Kharagpur and the Institute especially the mechanical engineering department of IIT Kharagpur for constantly supporting us.
