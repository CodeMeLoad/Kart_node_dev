The Team>>
Click to see full team structure.
Formula SAE at IIT Kharagpur took a solid form in October 2008, when a group of enthusiastic students got together to form TeamKART.
It was started up as a project under the aegis of the Sponsored Research and Industrial Consultancy Cell (SRIC) of IIT Kharagpur and under the purview of the Mechanical Department. Since then there has been a steady rise in our workforce and our potential as a team.
Our journey began with our first car, the KX-1. After that we, in the span of less than 2 years, achieved the feat of competing in an international event with our second car, the K-1. This was followed by K-2 which was lighter than all of its predecessors. K-2 was the first to participate in dynamic events, in FSAE 2013. We had our first podium in Formula Design Challenge 2015.
We are guided by our extremely competent faculty from the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Our faculty advisors are:
  • Prof. Amiya R. Mohanty
  • Prof. Kingshook Bhattacharyya
  • Prof Cheruvu S. Kumar
  • Prof Sushanta K. Panda
Cars
Our first car, the KX-1, was completed in 18 months. We participated in Formula Student 2010 thus making us the only team from Eastern India and amongst the few Indian teams to be a part of this prestigious event. We stood 9th in the cost report analysis and were amongst the 6 shortlisted teams for the Airbus Team Work Award.
 
Having completed our second car, the K-1, in just 7 months, we participated in Formula Student 2011. We are amongst the top 3 rookie teams at the event. Just like the previous year, we were once again nominated for the Airbus Team Work Award
 
 
We started designing our our third car, the K-2, in the December of 2011. The manufacture of the car took us about 5 months and we began its testing in the June of 2012. We participated in Formula Student 2013 where we stood 67th overall.
 
 
In 2015 with the now upgraded and rechristened K-2.2, we participated in Formula Design Challenge 2015 held at Coimbatore, where we brought home silverware in Cost Report and Business presentation events.
